Description:      A futuristic female playermodel

		  
====================================================
Further informations:

This model was originally build for a Quake3Arena Mod, called Project2501 (never released).
It is fully textured, but no animations. 
The mesh is seperated in three parts for Quake3Arena use.
Never mind the wasted texturespace... It is old, unpolished stuff ;)
